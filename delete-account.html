Delete Your ForkFeed Account

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your ForkFeed account and the data associated with it. ForkFeed is published by EvoTech Innovation.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open ForkFeed and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and associated personal data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address associated with your account at evotechnologiesinnovation@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my ForkFeed account". We will verify your request and delete your account.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile (email, name, profile picture, display name).
  • Your content: recipes, ingredients, steps, meal plans, reminders, grocery lists, notes, and uploaded photos.
  • Associated app activity tied to your account.

Data retention

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We remove your associated personal data promptly, except for limited records we must retain to comply with legal, tax, or fraud-prevention obligations. Requests are typically completed within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about account or data deletion? Contact us at evotechnologiesinnovation@gmail.com.
